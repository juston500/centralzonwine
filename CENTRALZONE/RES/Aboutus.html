About us

At Central Zone Wine, we are committed to producing wines that capture the essence of Dodoma's unique terroir. Our dedication to quality and innovation ensures that every bottle we produce reflects our mission: to bring people together through the shared experience of great wine.

Our Story

Fredrick Mbise, an entrepreneur with a deep appreciation for fine wine and a love for his community, established Central Zone Wine with a vision to elevate Tanzania's presence on the global wine stage. Starting with a small team and a big dream, we have grown into a trusted brand, known for our unwavering dedication to excellence.

Our Wines

We take pride in crafting a diverse range of wines, each carefully produced using locally sourced grapes from Dodoma’s fertile soils. Our collection features red, white, and rosé wines that are perfect for any occasion, whether you're celebrating a special moment or enjoying a quiet evening at home.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create world-class wines that showcase the best of Tanzanian agriculture while fostering sustainability and community development. We believe in supporting local farmers and empowering our community by providing opportunities for growth and prosperity.

Why Choose Us?

  • Quality: We adhere to the highest standards of winemaking, ensuring that every bottle is a testament to our commitment to excellence.
  • Local Roots: We are proud to be a Tanzanian brand that champions local talent and resources.
  • Sustainability: Our practices are designed to protect the environment and support the well-being of our community.

Experience the magic of Central Zone Wine by visiting our facilities in Dodoma. Join us for a tour and tasting session to discover the artistry and passion behind our wines.
